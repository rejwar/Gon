Let's Explore

Blockchain is a decentralized, digital ledger technology that records transactions across multiple computers in a secure, transparent, and immutable way. Each transaction is stored in a “block,” and these blocks are linked together in chronological order, forming a “chain.” The technology is most commonly associated with cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, but it has a wide range of applications in fields like supply chain management, finance, healthcare, and more
